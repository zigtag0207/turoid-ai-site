Why now

Documents are the hidden system

Many family office workflows are buried in PDFs and emails. AI becomes useful when it turns those documents into controlled, reviewable data for reporting, reconciliation, and CIO decisions.

Control

AI should assist, not invent

For family offices, AI document processing must stay grounded in source evidence. Turoid is built to support extraction, review, reporting, and workflow actions with links back to the underlying documents.

Document AI checklist

  • Source file retained with extracted values
  • Human review before reporting or workflow action
  • Output mapped to portfolio and fund records
  • Exceptions routed to the right owner
  • Searchable history for CIO and operations teams
  • Evidence-backed answers, not generic AI summaries
