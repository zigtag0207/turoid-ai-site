Primary CTA: Book a free 20-minute HK family office software audit.

Secondary CTA: Download the HK Family Office Software Stack Benchmark 2026.

## Week 3: Proof And Comparison

Actions:

- Publish one founder post on Excel risk and one on AI document controls.
- Send a short comparison note to prospects who clicked or replied: spreadsheets vs global reporting platforms vs Turoid.
- Offer the audit as the bridge: `Before buying a platform, benchmark one workflow.`

Comparison framing:

- Spreadsheets are flexible, but person-dependent.
- Global reporting platforms are powerful, but can be heavy if the immediate pain is one workflow.
- Turoid starts with controlled reporting, document, alternatives, and approval workflows where AI can safely reduce manual work.

## Week 4: Convert To Pilots

Actions:

- For each audit conversation, produce a one-page bottleneck map.
- Recommend one 30-day pilot: one report, one document type, one private fund workflow, one approval flow, or one set of CIO questions.
- Follow up with a concrete implementation outline and next meeting.

Audit output template:

1. Current workflow reviewed
2. Monthly or quarterly pain
3. Source systems and documents
4. Manual checks or control gaps
5. Practical next step
6. Suggested 30-day pilot
