# Reconciliation Software for Family Offices

Turoid is built to reduce reconciliation drift across custodians, banks, administrators, and manager statements—so reporting outputs are explainable and repeatable.

**CTA:** Request a demo  
Also see: `/family-office-software/`

## What needs reconciling in a family office

- Positions and holdings
- Transactions and corporate actions
- Cash and balances
- FX and multi-currency reporting implications
- Valuations (especially for alternatives)
- Document-backed inputs (notices, statements, capital calls)

## Common sources of breaks

- Timing differences for alternatives and NAV updates
- Inconsistent identifiers across banks/custodians
- Corporate actions and reclassifications
- Manual adjustments that never get re-applied consistently

## A practical reconciliation workflow

1. Ingest and normalize sources
2. Match positions/transactions/cash
3. Route exceptions to owners
4. Require approvals for adjustments
5. Preserve an audit trail (what changed, why, and who approved)

See controls: `/family-office-controls-approvals/`

## FAQ

### How do exceptions get handled?
Breaks are tracked as exceptions with owners, actions, and resolution notes, forming an auditable trail.

### Do you support multiple currencies?
Yes—multi-currency portfolios require consistent FX treatment and clear reporting views.
