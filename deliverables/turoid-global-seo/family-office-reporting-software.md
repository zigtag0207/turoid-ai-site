# Family Office Reporting Software

Turoid helps wealth teams produce reliable reporting without spreadsheet drift—by tying reporting outputs to reconciled data and repeatable workflows.

**CTA:** Request a demo  
Also see: `/family-office-software/`

## Why family office reporting breaks

- Multiple custodians and inconsistent statement formats
- Alternative investments with delayed valuations and document-based data
- Multi-entity structures and principal-level views
- Manual mapping, roll-forwards, and late-night copy/paste before IC packs

## Reporting outputs teams typically need

- Monthly / quarterly consolidated reporting packs
- Entity and principal views
- Strategy / manager / account breakdowns
- Cashflow and contribution tracking
- Exceptions and data-quality notes attached to reports

## How Turoid reporting works

1. Ingest and normalize data sources
2. Reconcile before publishing outputs
3. Generate repeatable pack structures
4. Track changes and maintain an audit trail

## Alternatives support (practically)

Reporting for alternatives depends on documents and timing. Turoid is designed to turn alternative investment document inflow into structured, reviewable data and workflows.

See: `/family-office-document-ai/`

## FAQ

### What reports can you generate?
Monthly and quarterly consolidated packs, with views by entity, principal, manager, strategy, and currency.

### How do you handle alternative investments?
By integrating document workflows, cashflows, valuations, and exception handling so reporting can be produced consistently.

### Do you reconcile before reporting?
Yes—reporting should be downstream of reconciliation so outputs are explainable and auditable.
