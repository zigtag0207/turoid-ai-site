# Controls & Approvals for Family Office Operations

Family offices need governance and operational controls across data changes, exception handling, and reporting publication. Turoid is designed to make controls repeatable and auditable.

**CTA:** Request a demo  
Also see: `/family-office-software/`

## What “controls” mean in a family office

- Clear owners and approval paths
- Audit trails for changes and adjustments
- Repeatable operating procedures (not ad hoc heroics)
- Exceptions tracked and resolved before reporting is published

## Where approvals matter most

- Reconciliation adjustments and overrides
- Data corrections that affect reporting outputs
- Exceptions that require sign-off before closing a period

Controls only matter if they connect to reconciliation and reporting outputs.
