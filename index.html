FAQ

Questions people ask when looking for family office software

This section helps both buyers and search engines understand where Turoid fits: portfolio operations, reporting, controls, and AI-assisted investment workflows.

What is Turoid?

Turoid is family office software for Hong Kong and Singapore wealth management teams that need better portfolio operations, reporting, reconciliation, trade controls, and AI-assisted decision support.

Who is Turoid built for?

Turoid is built for family offices, multi family offices, private investment offices, and wealth teams managing complex portfolios across multiple data sources.

What makes CIO Copilot different?

CIO Copilot sits on top of your own data, manager notes, event calendars, and workflows so answers are grounded in your operating reality, not generic market commentary.

Does Turoid replace existing custodians and private banks?

No. Turoid is designed to work with existing custodians, private banks, market data sources, and document workflows so teams can modernize without a total reset.

Does Turoid serve Hong Kong family offices?

Yes. Turoid is based in Sheung Wan, Hong Kong and is built for family offices, multi family offices, and private investment teams operating across Hong Kong, Singapore, and global custodian relationships.

What should HK family offices look for in software?

Look for cross-bank data consolidation, alternative investment reporting, document intelligence, approval controls, and AI workflows that stay grounded in your own portfolio and operating context.
