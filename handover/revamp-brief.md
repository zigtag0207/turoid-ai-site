# Turoid Website Revamp Brief

## Current Positioning

Turoid is family office software and agentic AI for Hong Kong, Singapore, and global wealth teams. The site should feel credible to principals, CIOs, investment operations leads, and private wealth teams who deal with fragmented portfolio data, reporting, documents, alternatives, Excel workflows, approvals, and AI adoption.

Simple positioning:

> Turoid helps family offices modernize reporting, Excel-heavy workflows, alternative investment operations, document processing, controls, and AI-assisted CIO work.

## Revamp Objective

Make the website feel more polished, premium, and conversion-focused without losing the current SEO momentum.

The redesign should help a visitor quickly understand:

- What Turoid does.
- Who it is for.
- What pain it solves.
- Why it is credible.
- What action to take next.

## Target Audience

Primary:

- Hong Kong family offices.
- Multi family offices.
- Private investment offices.
- Wealth operations teams.
- CIOs, COOs, CFOs, principals, and investment operations leads.

Secondary:

- Family office advisers.
- External asset managers.
- Private wealth consultants.
- Fund administrators and professional service providers serving family offices.

## Design Direction

The site should feel:

- Private-wealth credible.
- Calm and precise.
- Operationally intelligent.
- Premium, but not flashy.
- AI-native, but not gimmicky.

Avoid:

- Generic SaaS gradients.
- Crypto/startup hype.
- Overly playful illustrations.
- Claims that sound too broad or unproven.
- Removing useful content just to make the page shorter.

## Messaging Themes To Keep

- Family offices usually have a workflow problem before they have a software problem.
- Reporting, Excel, alternatives, documents, and controls are the practical starting points.
- AI is useful when grounded in controlled data, evidence, review queues, and workflow context.
- Turoid is built for private wealth operations, not generic enterprise automation.

## Primary Calls To Action

Use these conversion paths:

- Book a free HK family office software audit: `/hk-family-office-software-audit/`
- Download the HK software stack benchmark: `/hk-family-office-software-stack-benchmark/`
- Take the readiness assessment: `/hk-family-office-software-readiness-assessment/`

## Homepage Revamp Priorities

Recommended first-screen content:

- Clear headline around family office software and AI workflows.
- Subcopy mentioning reporting, alternatives, Excel, document AI, controls, and CIO workflows.
- Primary CTA: book free audit.
- Secondary CTA: download benchmark or view HK software guide.
- Credibility signals: Hong Kong base, team, press, NVIDIA/HKSTP/PolyU logos if still accurate.

Recommended homepage sections:

- Pain and workflow map.
- Product/workflow capabilities.
- AI document and CIO Copilot explanation.
- HK benchmark/audit lead magnet.
- Team and credibility.
- Contact CTA.

## SEO Constraints

The site has existing search visibility for HK family office software terms. Preserve the existing content architecture unless Nick approves a deliberate migration.

Do not remove or rename these high-value pages without redirects:

- `/family-office-software-hong-kong/`
- `/hk-family-office-software/`
- `/best-family-office-software-hong-kong/`
- `/family-office-reporting-software-hong-kong/`
- `/hk-family-office-software-audit/`
- `/hk-family-office-software-stack-benchmark/`
- `/hk-family-office-software-readiness-assessment/`

## Approval Standard

The revamp is ready to go live only when:

- Desktop and mobile look polished.
- Core CTAs are visible and working.
- No SEO pages are broken or accidentally removed.
- Schema, canonical tags, sitemap, robots, and `llms.txt` remain coherent.
- Nick has approved the preview URL.
