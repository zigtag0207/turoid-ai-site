Signals

Signs your family office has outgrown Excel

Risk

Bank statements, PDFs, and fund files are manually copied into month-end spreadsheets.

Scale

Control

Migration

Replace the operating burden, not the analyst workflow

A good family office Excel replacement does not ban spreadsheets. It moves repeatable, controlled work into software while still allowing exports, analysis, and bespoke views when the team needs them.
