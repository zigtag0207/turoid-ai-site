FAQ

Questions about family office software

Clear answers for teams evaluating category fit, workflows, and implementation reality.

What is family office software?

Software that consolidates portfolio data, supports reporting and operational workflows, and adds controls so teams can run repeatable processes.

Is it the same as portfolio management software?

No. Portfolio tools focus on analytics; family office software also needs reconciliation, document handling, controls, and reporting workflows across entities.

How do you handle alternatives?

Alternatives are document- and timing-driven. Turoid is designed to incorporate documents, cashflows, valuations, and exception handling so reporting stays consistent.

Does Turoid replace banks or custodians?

No. Turoid is designed to work with existing custodians, private banks, administrators, and document workflows.
