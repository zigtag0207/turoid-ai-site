Hong Kong Context

Why generic dashboards are not enough

A family office dashboard may show positions, but Hong Kong teams also need operating workflows: document handling, exception reviews, approvals, multi-bank reconciliation, and reporting views for principals, CIOs, and operations.

Reporting checklist

  • Cross-bank position and cash views
  • Alternative investment capital activity
  • Source document evidence for reported numbers
  • Monthly reporting workflow status
  • Principal, CIO, and operations-ready views
  • AI-assisted commentary and follow-up tracking
